PALAWAN ISLAND, PHILIPPINES

About

Palawan is an island in the Philippines located at the countrys Mimaropa region. It is known for its crystal clear waters, limestone cliffs, and amazing scenery. Many also know this island as the "Last Frontier". Palawan is a must travel destination!

Top places to visit in Palawan

Coron

    Coron is one of the most popular places to go to in Palawan. This area is famous for its diving sites like Siete Pecados and Twin Lagoon.

El Nido

    Its Popular for the beautiful lagoons, hidden beaches, and bright marine life.

Puerto Princesa
